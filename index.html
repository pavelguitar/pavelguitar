About Me

During the last 7 years music has completely absorbed my life. I've been collaborating with other musicians, making creative covers of other songs and writing my own music.

In 2022 I've completed the Vocal Academy from the Complete Vocal Institute in Copenhagen and returned home to Helsinki.

Since then I've been making short educational videos on instagram, doing acoustic solo performances and learning other instruments.

Lessons

I have a huge passion for sharing my knowledge and would be happy to help you on your musical journey!

My goal is to get my students to understand all aspects of music and be able to make their own choices in composition, improvisation and performance.

I teach guitar and music production in Ableton Live via Zoom and in-person in Helsinki either at my home or in a studio.
